# <em>About</em>

I'm Pedro — a filmmaker in the making, working toward making films full-time. For now, I split my time between a remote job and the creative projects that fill my afternoons and nights.

This site is a public notebook. Here I record the process behind my films, essays on cinema and animation, and anything worth keeping. The intent is twofold: to leave a record for myself, and to offer something useful to anyone walking a similar path.

## What you'll find here

- **Making-ofs** — detailed accounts of each film's production, with the difficulties, decisions, and what I learned along the way.
- **Projects** — a living page with what's in development and what's already published.
- **Essays** — longer pieces on cinema, animation, narrative, and whatever else I feel like exploring.
